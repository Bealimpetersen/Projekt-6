How often do my antenna need testing?

Antennas would normally need testing before connected to a satellite. For the smaller antennas maybe one time, for bigger antennas they could easily be tested each year to make sure that they point correctly.

Does the drone adjust the antenna or do I do it manually afterwards?

The drone does not adjust the antenna. The QuadSAT system provides you with measurements and patterns which you can then use to calibrate your equipment.
